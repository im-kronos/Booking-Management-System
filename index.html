7.3/10

ABOUT US

     Filmhouse At Home is our new online booking platform, allowing you to enjoy the finest Filmhouse-curated
         cinema from the comfort of your own home.
    Initially offering 20+ titles starting from 500, our Filmhouse at Home programme will grow to present a similar
    selection of films to that which our audiences are accustomed to: independent, world cinema at its best.
    
    Live from Friday 26 March, our first titles include the award-winning Stray, a captivating documentary about stray dogs
    on the streets of Istanbul that had its premiere at London Film Festival, Holocaust survival drama Persian Lessons from
    the director of House of Sand and Fog, Vadim Perelman, critically-acclaimed documentary Poly Styrene: I am a Cliché
    about counter-culture icon and punk pioneer Poly Styrene and the first documentary entirely in Scots Gaelic, Iorram
    (Boat Song) which is a lyrical portrait of the fishing community in Outer Hebrides, past and present. Our future
    programme will include, among others, Sundance-winning and now Oscar®-nominated Minari.
    
    You can sign up to use our services for free. You will only pay for the films you choose to book. Simply create an
    account and browse!
    
    Please visit our Help & FAQs section for more information on Filmhouse at Home.
    
    Filmhouse at Home is generously supported by the Weston Cultural Fund.
